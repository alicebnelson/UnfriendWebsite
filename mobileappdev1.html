Mobile App Development

Why Mobile Application Development?

In today’s market, any business can reap the benefits of building a mobile app tailored specifically to clients’ needs. The average American spends about two hours each day on their smartphones—and out of those two hours, they spend 86% of their time switching between mobile apps. With more and more consumers switching to smartphones for online searches and purchases, creating a social presence through mobile apps is a crucial step for a company of any size. A custom app from our team helps you stay on your client’s phones—and on their minds.

Step Beyond Mobile Websites

Although your business’s mobile website can help to create a responsive and user-friendly interface for clients, it doesn’t provide the streamlined convenience of an app customized to their needs. Mobile websites force users to search within their browser for a business’s site, waiting for the page to load at the rate of their connection speed. Alternatively, an app can be opened directly, and customers often retain some functionality even without an internet connection.

An App for Every Business. Even Yours.

No matter what products or services your business offers, there’s an app for that. With a clear interface designed to make the user experience as straightforward and focused as possible, a mobile app can effectively display a range of information, from prices, booking forms, and menus to messaging, banking, and news.

Build Brand Recognition and Loyalty.

For businesses of any size, our mobile apps are designed to further your ongoing branding initiatives. A lean, aesthetically pleasing app branded with your company’s logo ensures that your message is always right at your clients’ fingertips. Create customer loyalty by going beyond traditional advertising, and by showing clients that your company is prepared to meet them where they are, simplifying their lives with a streamlined and user-friendly experience.

With years of experience designing custom mobile apps for businesses just like yours, Unfriend Studios, LLC can create the app you need. Contact us to learn how we can help.
